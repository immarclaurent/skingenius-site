Privacy & Terms

How we protect your data and the terms that govern use of SkinGenius.

Last updated: June 2026

Privacy policy

SkinGenius is committed to protecting personal and health information. This summary explains what we collect and how we handle it. It is provided for general information and is not legal advice.

Information we collect

  • Account data — name, email, and organization for users who register.
  • Image & analysis data — images submitted for analysis and the results generated.
  • Usage data — technical logs used to operate and secure the service.

How we use information

  • To provide skin-analysis results and operate the platform.
  • To secure, monitor, and improve service reliability.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

We do not use patient images to train our models without explicit consent, and we never sell personal data.

How we protect it

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).
  • Role-based access controls and full audit logging.
  • Regional data residency options (US / EU).
  • Handling aligned with HIPAA and GDPR safeguards.

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact contact@skingenius.app.

Data retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the service and meet legal obligations, after which it is deleted or anonymized.

Terms of use

Acceptable use

SkinGenius is intended for use by qualified healthcare professionals and authorized organizations. You agree not to misuse the service, attempt to reverse engineer it, or use it in violation of applicable law.

Medical disclaimer

SkinGenius is a clinical decision-support tool. It does not provide a medical diagnosis and is not a substitute for professional medical advice, examination, or treatment. Always rely on the judgment of a qualified clinician.

Limitation of liability

The service is provided "as is" without warranties of any kind to the fullest extent permitted by law. SkinGenius is not liable for clinical decisions made using the platform.

Changes

We may update these terms and this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the service or by email.

Questions about privacy or these terms? Email contact@skingenius.app.
