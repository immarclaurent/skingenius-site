About

Clarity for every skin, everywhere

SkinGenius exists to make dermatologist-level skin analysis fast, equitable, and trustworthy — so the right care reaches more people, sooner.

Our mission

Skin disease is one of the most common reasons people seek medical care, yet access to dermatology is deeply uneven. Wait times stretch for months, specialists are concentrated in cities, and diagnostic accuracy varies across skin tones. We believe well-built AI can help close that gap — not by replacing clinicians, but by extending their reach.

What we do

SkinGenius is a clinical decision-support platform that analyzes images of skin conditions in seconds and returns an explainable, evidence-backed assessment. It helps clinicians triage faster, supports primary care and telehealth teams, and gives patients in underserved areas a clearer path to the care they need.

What we stand for

Patients first

Every decision starts with whether it helps a real person get better care.

Evidence over hype

We validate against clinical ground truth and publish how our models perform.

Privacy by default

Health data is sensitive. We protect it with encryption and strict controls.

Equity across skin tones

We test fairness across all six Fitzpatrick types to reduce performance gaps.

Built with clinicians

SkinGenius is developed alongside board-certified dermatologists, primary care physicians, and AI researchers. Clinical oversight is part of our process — from how we label data to how we communicate uncertainty in every result.
