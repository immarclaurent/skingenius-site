Frequently asked questions

Is SkinGenius a medical diagnosis?

No. SkinGenius is a clinical decision-support tool intended to assist qualified healthcare professionals. It does not provide a definitive diagnosis and is not a substitute for in-person evaluation, biopsy, or professional medical judgment.

How accurate is the model?

In internal validation across 30+ conditions, SkinGenius achieves 99.2% top-1 accuracy on held-out clinical datasets, with performance audited across all six Fitzpatrick skin types. We publish per-condition sensitivity and specificity to enterprise customers under NDA.

How do you protect patient data?

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256), processed under HIPAA and GDPR safeguards, and stored in your chosen region. Patient images are never used to train models without explicit consent.

Can it integrate with our EHR or telehealth platform?

Yes. Our REST and FHIR-compatible API, plus SDKs for web and mobile, integrate with most EHR and telehealth systems in days. Sandbox credentials are available the moment you’re approved.

Is it cleared by regulators?

SkinGenius operates under an ISO 13485 quality management system. Regulatory clearance (e.g., FDA, CE-MDR) varies by region and intended use; our team will walk you through the appropriate pathway for your deployment.
